Irish Baba

Year: 2025

Genre: Comedy/Drama

Irish Baba is a comedy satire about a self-proclaimed spiritual guru in Ireland who navigates deception, faith, and unexpected twists. Meanwhile, Namita, an accomplice of Irish Baba, with secrets of her own, becomes entangled in the web of manipulation, leading to an unexpected power struggle. Blending humor with intrigue, the film explores the thin line between belief and illusion.
