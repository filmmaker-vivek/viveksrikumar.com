Sheee

Year: 2026

Genre: Genre-Blend

Sheee is a haunting Irish folklore drama that blurs the line between myth and reality. When Sean, a man searching for meaning, joins Kumar, a folklore enthusiast obsessed with local legends, their curiosity leads them deep into the woods- in search of Dubheasa, a woman once believed to be cursed and who disappeared centuries ago. What begins as a curious night of exploration turns into a journey across time- where love, guilt, and myth intertwine.
