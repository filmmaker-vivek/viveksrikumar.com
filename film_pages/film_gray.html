Gray

Year: 2023

Genre: Horror/Drama

A psychological drama that follows a womans's weird experiences in her apartment. Struggling with loss and isolation, she is trapped in a complex web of emotions involving her roommate and is pushed to confront her own perceptions.

Awards and Recognitions: Best LGBT Film (Rameshwaram International Film Festival), Best Indian Short Film (Cuckoo International Film Awards), Best LGBT Short Film (Grand Pixel International Film Festival). Best LGBTQ Short Film (Mokkho International Film Festival), Best Composer [ International & Indian Short film] (Parai International Musical Awards), Best Indian Short Film (Tamizhagam International Film Festival), Best Asian short Film (Indo Singapore International Film Festival), Best Short film (Kookai International Film Festival)
